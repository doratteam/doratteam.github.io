About DoratTeam

DoratTeam is a team of hackers/designers from Northwestern University in Evanston, IL.

We try to put together some cool projects every once in a while, and we're always looking for more ideas to deploy and bring brilliant ideas to real life.

The Team

Portfolios

The Hungry Wildcats

We created an app to help Northwestern students decide what to eat when they just can't decide. Put in the amount you'd like to spend for dinner, and we'll randomly find restaurants that's within a walkable distance from Northwestern campus in Evanston.

Mandal-Art Generator

Mandal art is a technique developed by a Japanese designer. It helps you to brainstorm an idea, by forcing you to generate 8 different ways to achieve something, and then again thinking about 8 different ways to achieve each of those ways. We made this so that you can easily set your goal and think about how you might achieve that goal.
